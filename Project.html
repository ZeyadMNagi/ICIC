Stage 1: Idea


When people think about helping others, they often get confused about how to do so. This is exactly what happened to us, but instead of giving up, we started learning and searching for answers.
We were shocked to learn that 10% of deaf and mute people are in Egypt, and the ways to help them are very limited.

That's when we decided to take action and make communication with them easier. After considering many ideas, we finally came up with the concept of creating The Sign Suit.

Stage 2: Hardware


After finding our target, we began working to make it a reality.

We started searching for the right materials to develop our project and where to begin. With guidance from experienced mentors, we identified the correct materials and started purchasing the hardware.

Now that we have all the hardware, we've encountered a problem - it doesn't do anything.

Stage 3: Software


To get the project up and running, we needed to program the Arduino, but none of us knew how to code.
Two of our team members dedicated two weeks to learning how to code the Arduino, fueled by a lot of coffee and intense focus.
